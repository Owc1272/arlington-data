Class Size & Staffing

  • Student-teacher ratio moved from 13.6 (2007-08) to 12.1 (2025-26), converging with the peer average.

Academic Performance

  • SAT Math scores moved from 554 (2006-07) to 630 (2024-25). Note: SAT was redesigned in 2016-17.

Student Outcomes

  • 4-year college rate: 69.6% (1994-95) to 87.4% (2024-25) — 9 pts above the peer average.
  • AP test takers nearly quadrupled: 191 (2007) to 735 (2025).

Teacher Compensation

  • Arlington salary $95,661 is $4,143 below the peer average ($99,804).
  • But total salary bill quadrupled ($12.5M → $46.9M) due to 80% growth in teacher FTEs.

School Spending & Workforce

  • Per-pupil expenditure rose from $11,685 to $20,549 (+76%), but Arlington remains 9th of 13 peers.
  • Teacher FTE grew 80% (272 → 490) while enrollment grew more modestly.

Student Demographics

  • The student body has become significantly more diverse over the past decade.
