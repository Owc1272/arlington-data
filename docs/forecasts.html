Ottoson: What History Tells Us About the True Cost

Source: Arlington Capital Planning Committee Reports, MSBA data
Ottoson Middle School (built 1921, last renovated 1996-97) is 105 years old. AHS completion (Oct 2025) opens the door to submit a Statement of Interest to MSBA — the April 2026 deadline is the first realistic window.

  • AHS pattern: $100M estimate (2014) → $291M final (2019) = 2.9x escalation
  • Current MA costs: Actual school bids now exceed $800/SF; Lexington HS at $659M, Madison Park at $700M
  • Realistic Ottoson range: $200M–$350M total, with ~70% ($140M–$245M) borne locally
  • Timeline: SOI → feasibility (913 days) → design → construction. Earliest completion: 2033–2035
